Who are the Espians?

Founded in 1999 by tav, we are a global collective of visionaries, technologists, artists, engineers and cultural creatives.

What are our goals?

We are committed to drive social, environmental and economic prosperity through open source and decentralised systems that enables individuals to improve their own lives and communities.

We are on a mission to deliver on the answers to the following 3 questions:

  1. How can we all be empowered to do what we love and live comfortably?
  2. How can we effectively organise people, ideas and resources?
  3. How can we be united in our diversity so that culture may truly flourish?
